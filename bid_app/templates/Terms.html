How the Auctions Work

With each bid placed, the price of the item on which the user is bidding increases by a small preset amount (10 paisa INR to 50 Paisa INR). Each new bid resets the remaining time for an auction up to the amount specified for the Auction Type (usually 10, 20, 30, 50, and 60 seconds or up to 10 Minutes as specified for that particular auction).
When a bid is placed, number of credits are deducted from the user’s bidding account as per the specified number of credits for that auction. Placed bids are not refundable. An auction closes when no more bids are received by Bidderboy’ servers. The countdown time is indicated for each auction. Due to the time it takes for bids to reach Bidderboy’ servers, and in order to allow all users the same opportunity to bid regardless of geographic location, Bidderboy’ servers will wait for an additional 5 seconds for any placed bids to reach Bidderboy servers before closing the auction.
The user who was the last to place a bid wins the auction and can purchase the product.
Bids become valid when they are entered in the Bidderboy database and registered in Bidderboy’ system. Bidderboy is not responsible for disruptions in the user’s connection and errors that may occur as a result of this. This includes, but is not limited to, incorrect countdown of the remaining time, outdated information on bidders, and bids.

Buy Now Option

We may, at our sole discretion, also offer users a "Buy Now" option. The Buy Now option allows a user to buy the auctioned product using up to 89% of the value of the bids already spent in that auction. It gives the user the option to purchase the product by paying the difference between the value of the spent bids in that auction and the product value, which is the suggested retail price. The user may only use the value of the bids he/she has placed in the auction up to the product value. If the value of the placed bids is higher than the product value, then the bids in excess of the product value cannot be used in a Buy Now purchase. The value of any bids spent above the value of the product will not be refunded. The Buy Now purchase price can never be below zero. The user can buy the product using the Buy Now option at any time when the auction is online and up to 24 hours after the auction has closed. When a user decides to buy the product by exercising the Buy Now option, he/she may no longer participate in the auction, any AutoBids that have been placed in that auction will be cancelled and the remaining AutoBid bids returned to the user’s bid account. The auction will continue uninterrupted for all other users. All purchases pursuant to a Buy Now option shall be subject to the user’s payment in full of all amounts due for the product (including all shipping and handling charges) within 24 hours after the auction ends. Unless payment is received in full within this time, the user’s right and Buy Now option to purchase and receive the product is automatically withdrawn and cancelled, and the user shall not be entitled to a refund of any bids placed.
