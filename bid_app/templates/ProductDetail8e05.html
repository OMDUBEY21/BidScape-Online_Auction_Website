 Description

Groove into music anywhere and anytime with the Philips BT50A/00 wireless portable speaker. Enjoy crisp music with impeccable bass with Philips trusted sound system and anti-clipping function for loud and distortion free music. Do not get tangled up with wires, stream music easily via Bluetooth and easy connectivity to almost any media device. Grab the compact speakers and make one of those long drives a musical fest.

Design

The Philips BT50A/00 wireless portable speaker has a very compact design [6.1x 6.1 x 7.6 cm (WxLxH)] perfectly designed for people on the move. Their “grab and go” design with vibrant colours and a smooth rubber finish is built to please your eyes and also grab attention of others. At 91g the speakers are very light and can fit easily in to any bag without any hassle.

Bluetooth Connectivity

Connect to any device Bluetooth enabled device with Philips BT50A/00 portable speaker running Bluetooth 4.0 technology. These durable speakers are compatible with iPads, iPhones, Android tablets and smartphones. The Bluetooth has a range of up to 10 metres and supports profiles of A2DP, AVRCP and HPF.

Aux and USB Port

The Philips BT50A/00 portable speaker comes with a standard 3.5 mm audio-in and a USB port that doubles up as the charging port.

Power

Do not be fooled by the Philips BT50A/00’s size, the long-lasting speakers may look small but it delivers crystal clear music even at loud volumes. It has a total RMS output of 3W providing a well balance treble and bass.

Technical Features

Power usage of this device is low with only 2W power output – making its batteries last long.

Battery (Life, Charging time, Battery type)

Provided with a built-in Li-Po batteries and 2W power output, the Philips BT50A/00 wireless portable speaker makes the best use of power by consuming less and delivering more. Once charged the device has a playback time of up to 6 hours and it takes only 1-2 hours for it to be fully charged and ready for your next 6 hours music session.

Wireless Playback

Enjoy wireless playback with Bluetooth 4.0 technology with a connectivity range of up to 10 metres.

Sound Quality

Philips innovation is exhibited in these small speakers with the adoption of Anti-Clipping feature which prevents distortion in sound – ensuring clear sound at high volumes. The Philips BT50A/00 wireless portable speaker built-in amplifiers and a 3.81 cm driver work towards providing exquisite sound clarity and a surround sound feel.

Know What Is Inside the Packed Box

The sales package comes with the Philips BT50A/00 wireless portable speaker, a USB cable, 3.5 mm AUX connection line, Warranty leaflet and a User guide.
